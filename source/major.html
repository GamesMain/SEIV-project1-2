How can I be well prepared to be a Computer Science major?


If you like problem solving, and if you like math, and you like learning in general… you’ll enjoy Computer Science. There are many free online resources that can teach the basic coding skills and commands, some are listed in the Resources page. There are also many events like game jams, coding contests and International Game Day that will give you the chance to practice.



What is being a Computer Science major like?


Once you've started going to school as a Computer Science major there will be lots of schoolwork and assignments for your classes. Most assignments will build off of your knowledge and skills that you learned in previously courses and assignments. As soon as you finish one, there will be another one after it so you have to learn to schedule your time and prioritize your work load and calendar to achieve your goal of finishing your degree.
